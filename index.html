What can you expect at NIFT Craft Bazaar?

Immerse yourself in the vibrant ambiance as the NIFT Craft Bazaar showcases a diverse range of handmade creations, bringing together talented artisans, designers, and craftsmen from across the country. With its focus on promoting traditional techniques and contemporary designs, this event is a celebration of India's rich cultural heritage and its thriving creative community

Artisanal Exhibitions: Explore a captivating array of handcrafted products ranging from exquisite textiles, intricate jewelry, stunning ceramics, enchanting paintings, and so much more. Witness the sheer craftsmanship and attention to detail that goes into each unique creation.

Live Demonstrations: Gain insights into the traditional techniques and artistic processes involved in various crafts as skilled artisans provide live demonstrations. Marvel at their skill and dedication, and even try your hand at some of these time-honored techniques.

Engaging Workshops: Unlock your own creative potential by participating in interactive workshops conducted by experts. Learn new skills, experiment with different mediums, and create your very own masterpiece under the guidance of skilled artisans.

Fashion Show Extravaganza: Be mesmerized by the fusion of traditional crafts and contemporary fashion as designers showcase stunning ensembles that blend heritage craftsmanship with modern aesthetics. Witness the magic of art coming to life on the runway. Food and Cultural Delights: Indulge your senses in a culinary journey, savoring delicious traditional delicacies from different regions of India. Immerse yourself in the cultural tapestry of our diverse nation through captivating music, dance performances, and cultural showcases.

Shopping Galore: Take home a piece of creativity and support the artisans directly by shopping from a wide selection of handcrafted products. From unique home decor items to fashionable accessories, find the perfect treasure that reflects your personal style.

Whether you are an art enthusiast, a connoisseur of craftsmanship, or simply looking for an enriching and delightful experience, the NIFT Craft Bazaar is a must-visit event. Mark your calendars and join us at the NIFT Craft Bazaar for an unforgettable celebration of artistry and creativity. Let the magic of handmade treasures inspire you and ignite your passion for all things artistic. See you there!
